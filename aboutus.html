About Us

 

The location of Sri Guru Prasad Inter College Pathrauli is Fatteypur, Hardoi.The year of establishment of Sri Guru Prasad Inter College Pathrauli is 1999.

Sri Guru Prasad Inter College Pathrauli is a Co-Educational school. The student teacher ratio of Sri Guru Prasad Inter College Pathrauli can be approximated to be 1 teacher for every INF students.

It is a Sr. Secondary School (Class 9-12), where lowest class is 9th and highest class is 12th.

The school has Private building. It has got 0 classrooms for instructional purposes. All the classrooms are in good condition. It has 2 other rooms for non-teaching activities. The school has a separate room for Head master/Teacher. The school has Pucca boundary wall.

The school has 2 boys toilet and it is functional. and 2 girls toilet and it is functional. The school has a playground.
